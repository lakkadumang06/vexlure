Textile & Garments

Textiles are woven into the very fabric of our identity.

Headquartered in Surat, Gujarat—one of the largest textile hubs in the world—Vexlure International stands at the heart of the global textile trade. Surat is renowned for its dynamic textile industry, with a legacy of craftsmanship, innovation, and large-scale production. Growing up in this vibrant ecosystem, our team has gained deep industry knowledge and a natural flair for identifying trends, sourcing quality fabrics, and delivering fashion-forward solutions.

At Vexlure, we specialize in a diverse range of textiles and garments, from everyday essentials to premium fashion fabrics. Whether it's raw fabrics, finished garments, or custom textile solutions, we provide quality, consistency, and a global standard of service.

Driven by creativity and backed by one of the most powerful supply chains in the textile world, we bring you products that combine tradition with innovation.
