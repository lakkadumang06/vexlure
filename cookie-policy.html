About Vexlure International Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing site usage, and assisting in our marketing efforts.

Privacy Policy

Essential Cookies

Analytics Cookies

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an ad. These cookies can share that information with other organizations or advertisers.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Here's how you can manage your preferences.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience using our website.

Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll be presented with a cookie consent banner where you can choose which types of cookies to accept or reject.

Third-Party Tools

You can also opt out of some third-party cookies directly through the ad network using tools like the Digital Advertising Alliance's WebChoices tool.
